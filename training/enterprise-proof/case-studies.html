Case Study Framework

Enterprise‑safe stories without client exposure.

Required Sections

  1. Problem context (industry, not name)
  2. Operational challenge
  3. Agents deployed
  4. KPIs improved
  5. Executive outcome

What not to include

  • Client names without written approval
  • Exact dollar figures
  • Proprietary data or prompts
