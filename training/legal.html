Legal & Compliance Boundary

This portal trains partners to operate inside enterprise-safe boundaries. Use the language below in every proposal and engagement.

MBCC Finternet Ops™ provides software tooling and education only.
We do not provide custody services. We do not execute trades. We do not provide investment advice. Outputs are operational monitoring, reporting, explainability, and readiness scenarios.

Approved positioning statements

  • “We provide operational confidence tooling for tokenized rails: monitoring, governance narratives, and executive reporting.”
  • “We help teams explain incidents and trends in audit-ready language.”
  • “We support readiness through operational stress scenarios.”

Statements to avoid

  • “We predict markets / guarantee returns.”
  • “We advise what to buy or sell.”
  • “We replace compliance or legal.”
  • “We custody assets.”
