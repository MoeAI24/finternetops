Short responses you can use on calls. Keep it simple. Keep it accurate. Keep it safe.

“Is this making decisions for us?”

No. These agents surface and summarize signals to support review. Decision authority stays with your teams and controls.

“Can you guarantee results?”

We don’t guarantee outcomes. We run a scoped pilot with agreed KPIs and measure impact in your environment.

“Will this create compliance risk?”

We design for human-in-the-loop, audit trails, and evidence-linked reporting. We do not provide legal advice or claim regulatory approval.

“Do you keep our data?”

Data handling is defined in the SOW. For static portal training, no data is collected. Production deployments follow client requirements and approved storage policies.

“Can we white-label this?”

Yes—under a partner/whitelabel agreement. Branding is allowed only within tier boundaries. The agent logic and core methodology remain MBCC IP.
