Executive Brief Generator

Transforms daily ops signals into executive-ready briefs: what changed, why it matters, what’s next.

Tier: Consultant / Sales Human-in-the-loop required Back to Catalog

What this agent does

“Executive Brief Generator turns complex ops signals into simple leadership clarity—fast, consistent, and evidence-linked.”

Best for
  • Leadership needing fast clarity
  • Programs reporting tokenized ops status weekly
  • Partners selling pilots and retainers
Bad fit / walk-away
  • Guaranteeing business outcomes
  • Replacing leadership judgment
  • Making decisions without evidence

Inputs & outputs

Inputs
  • Daily ops summaries
  • Exception + settlement highlights
  • Liquidity status indicators
  • Risk notes and evidence
Outputs
  • 5-bullet exec brief
  • Weekly narrative report
  • Board-safe status slide text (no charts)
  • Action questions for leadership
Note: Implementation specifics (prompts/config/orchestration) are gated by partner tier and certification (see IP Notice).

KPIs this agent improves

  • Time-to-brief creation
  • Consistency of reporting
  • Executive satisfaction
  • Reduction in meeting time (measured)

Human-in-the-loop boundaries

  • No promises or guarantees.
  • Only summarizes provided signals.
  • Human owners validate before distribution.
Approved language: “flags”, “surfaces”, “summarizes”, “supports review”. Avoid “decides”, “executes”, “guarantees”.

Where it fits (pilot → production)

  • Pilot: daily brief + weekly rollup
  • Production: cadence + KPI scorecards + narrative trend
  • Sales: proposal-ready brief examples
