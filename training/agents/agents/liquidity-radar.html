Liquidity Radar

Tracks intraday liquidity signals and highlights stress indicators in tokenized collateral flows.

Tier: Consultant / Operator Human-in-the-loop required Back to Catalog

What this agent does

“Liquidity Radar turns messy intraday signals into a clean, explainable status—so teams spot stress earlier and coordinate faster.”

Best for
  • Teams monitoring collateral/liquidity needs
  • Risk/treasury groups running intraday check-ins
  • Executives needing simple liquidity status
Bad fit / walk-away
  • Providing regulatory capital advice
  • Guaranteeing liquidity outcomes
  • No defined liquidity signals/inputs

Inputs & outputs

Inputs
  • Collateral movements (tokenized ledger)
  • Cashflow schedules (approved)
  • Intraday funding indicators
  • Policy thresholds (client-defined)
Outputs
  • Liquidity status dashboard summary
  • Stress indicator alerts (threshold-based)
  • Daily liquidity narrative (plain English)
  • 30/60/90 improvement notes
Note: Implementation specifics (prompts/config/orchestration) are gated by partner tier and certification (see IP Notice).

KPIs this agent improves

  • Time-to-signal liquidity stress
  • Coverage of monitored flows
  • Alert precision (tuned)
  • Executive comprehension (surveyed)

Human-in-the-loop boundaries

  • No capital adequacy determinations.
  • No regulatory advice.
  • All actions remain with treasury/risk owners.
Approved language: “flags”, “surfaces”, “summarizes”, “supports review”. Avoid “decides”, “executes”, “guarantees”.

Where it fits (pilot → production)

  • Pilot: daily status + threshold tuning
  • Production: alerts + daily narrative + weekly exec rollup
  • Audit: threshold change history
