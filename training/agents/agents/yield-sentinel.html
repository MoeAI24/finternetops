Market Ops • Yield Monitoring

Yield Sentinel

Monitors tokenized treasury yield signals and flags anomalies for human review.

Tier: Operator / Consultant Human-in-the-loop required Back to Catalog

What this agent does

“Yield Sentinel improves visibility into tokenized treasury yield movement and flags anomalies early—so your team can review faster and act with confidence.”

Best for
  • Asset managers monitoring intraday yield shifts
  • Treasury desks tracking auction/curve moves
  • Ops teams validating tokenized collateral pricing
Bad fit / walk-away
  • Anyone requesting guaranteed returns
  • Fully autonomous trading or portfolio decisions
  • No access to approved market data sources

Inputs & outputs

Inputs
  • Market data feeds (rates, curves, auction calendars)
  • Tokenized collateral ledger snapshots
  • Internal pricing/risk reports
  • News headlines (optional, cited)
Outputs
  • Yield change summary (daily/intraday)
  • Anomaly alerts with evidence links/citations
  • Watchlist of instruments and thresholds
  • Executive-ready 5-bullet brief
Note: Implementation specifics (prompts/config/orchestration) are gated by partner tier and certification (see IP Notice).

KPIs this agent improves

  • Time-to-detect yield anomalies
  • Analyst throughput (briefs/day)
  • False-positive rate (tunable thresholds)
  • Report latency reduction

Human-in-the-loop boundaries

  • Never recommends trades or positions.
  • Never promises returns.
  • All alerts require human validation before action.
Approved language: “flags”, “surfaces”, “summarizes”, “supports review”. Avoid “decides”, “executes”, “guarantees”.

Where it fits (pilot → production)

  • Pilot: daily yield brief + anomaly watch
  • Production: alerting + audit logs + weekly exec brief
  • Audit: evidence trail for why alerts fired
