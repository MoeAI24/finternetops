Compliance Explainer

Converts policy, controls, and workflow decisions into plain-language explanations with citations.

Tier: Consultant Human-in-the-loop required Back to Catalog

What this agent does

“Compliance Explainer makes controls understandable and audit-ready—without crossing into legal advice.”

Best for
  • Governance teams drafting control narratives
  • Ops teams needing ‘why’ documentation
  • Leaders who need simplified compliance summaries
Bad fit / walk-away
  • Legal advice
  • Creating policy without client approval
  • Any request to fabricate citations or authority

Inputs & outputs

Inputs
  • Approved policies and procedures
  • Control catalog / risk register (sanitized)
  • Workflow logs (approved)
  • Evidence links or documents (provided)
Outputs
  • Plain-language compliance narrative
  • Control mapping summary
  • Audit-ready FAQ set
  • Risk/limit statements for exec decks
Note: Implementation specifics (prompts/config/orchestration) are gated by partner tier and certification (see IP Notice).

KPIs this agent improves

  • Time to produce control narratives
  • Audit question turnaround time
  • Documentation consistency
  • Reduction in rework cycles

Human-in-the-loop boundaries

  • Not legal counsel.
  • No regulatory interpretation beyond provided policy.
  • Citations required for claims.
Approved language: “flags”, “surfaces”, “summarizes”, “supports review”. Avoid “decides”, “executes”, “guarantees”.

Where it fits (pilot → production)

  • Pilot: build 3 core narratives (settlement, exceptions, liquidity)
  • Production: monthly control updates + audit Q&A pack
  • Audit: evidence-linked narratives
