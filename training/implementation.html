Module 8 — Implementation & Consulting Delivery

Goal: You can deploy MBCC Finternet Ops™ as an enterprise pilot, operationalize outputs, and scale safely — while monetizing services.

Consulting delivery playbook

1) Discovery (Week 1)

  • Stakeholder mapping: Ops, Risk, Compliance, Exec sponsor
  • Define “trust metrics”: what must never fail, what must be explainable
  • Agree on templates: executive brief format + compliance narrative format

2) Pilot build (Weeks 2–3)

  • Pick 1–3 agents (recommended: Settlement Watchdog + Compliance Explainer + Executive Brief)
  • Set thresholds, ingest sources, define severity taxonomy
  • Run a tabletop simulation with Liquidity Stress

3) Go-live and cadence (Week 4)

  • Weekly leadership packet
  • Monthly governance report
  • Incident post‑mortem format locked

Monetization model (partners)

What you sell: operational clarity as a service — pilots, subscriptions, compliance reporting retainers, workshops, executive briefs, and enterprise integration packages.
