Partner Toolkit

Use these assets to deliver pilots, workshops, and executive reporting. Everything here is written for enterprise stakeholders.

1) Discovery questions (first client meeting)

  • What must never fail? (settlement, reporting, customer experience, compliance)
  • What must be explainable within 15 minutes of an incident?
  • Which stakeholders require weekly cadence reports?
  • What is your current incident workflow (tickets, on-call, approvals)?
  • What evidence format does audit prefer (tickets, logs, screenshots, sign-offs)?

2) Pilot package (recommended)

3) Workshop format (90 minutes)

  1. 20 min: Finternet + operational risk framing (Module 1 talk-track)
  2. 20 min: Current-state incident walkthrough
  3. 30 min: Liquidity Stress scenario + settlement dependency mapping
  4. 15 min: Output templates (exec brief + compliance narrative)
  5. 5 min: Next actions + pilot plan

4) “What we do” statement (use verbatim)

We provide Finternet Operations tooling: monitoring, explainability, governance reporting, and stress scenarios for tokenized asset rails. We do not provide custody, trading, or investment advice.

5) Deliverable templates (copy/paste)

Executive Brief (one page)

  • What changed (24h/7d)?
  • Why it matters
  • Impact scope
  • Actions + owners + deadlines
  • Risks remaining

Compliance Narrative

  • Event summary (plain English)
  • Timeline
  • Controls triggered
  • Mitigations applied
  • Evidence references
  • Lessons learned + control improvements
