Module — Module 4 — Compliance Explainer Agent

Goal: You can deploy, explain, and operationalize Module 4 — Compliance Explainer Agent as a consulting product.

What this agent is

Compliance Explainer converts technical events into governance-ready language. It creates consistent narratives, policy alignment notes, and audit artifacts that reduce confusion during incidents and improve regulatory readiness.

Core question it answers

“How do we explain what happened in plain English — with controls and accountability?”

Signals (what it watches)

  • Event flags: Receives signals from all agents (latency, failures, anomalies, stress triggers).
  • Policy checkpoints: Maps events to internal controls and policy requirements.
  • Change log correlation: Connects incidents to deployments, config changes, or vendor events.
  • Escalation state: Tracks severity, owner, mitigation, and closure evidence.

Outputs (what it produces)

  • Compliance Narrative: Plain-English story of the event: what/when/impact/mitigation.
  • Control Mapping Notes: Which controls were triggered, which were effective, and what gaps exist.
  • Audit Artifact Pack: Logs + timeline + approvals summary in an audit-friendly structure.
  • Board‑Safe Summary: High-level version for leadership without technical overload.

Operational workflow

  1. Define governance templates (narrative format, terminology, severity labels).
  2. During events: auto-draft narrative within minutes; update as facts mature.
  3. Attach evidence links: logs, dashboards, tickets, approvals.
  4. Publish a board-safe summary for leadership and a detailed artifact pack for audit.
  5. Archive in a consistent repository for future audits and recurring controls.

Client talk-track (enterprise safe)

Compliance Explainer is your governance translator. It turns machine events into audit-ready narratives and control mapping. That means faster clarity during incidents and higher confidence during audits — without drifting into financial advice.
