Track C • Sales / Channel

Sales / Channel Track

For resellers and business development. Package the offer, qualify buyers, handle objections, and position MBCC agents safely (no financial advice, no guaranteed outcomes).

Open Onboarding Open Toolkit Dashboard

Requirements

  • Pitches MBCC Finternet Ops in 60 seconds (value + limits).
  • Qualifies buyers: data readiness, stakeholders, risk appetite.
  • Handles compliance objections with approved language.
  • Sells pilot packages + ongoing retainer services.

Deliverables you must be able to produce

  • 1-page offer sheet (pilot + monthly ops)
  • Discovery call script + qualification checklist
  • Objection handling library
  • Executive email template (pilot proposal)

Recommended learning path

  1. Foundations → pass quiz
  2. Complete your track’s core modules
  3. Use Partner Toolkit templates to build a pilot package
  4. Deliver a mock executive readout (15 minutes)
  5. Take certification exam (80% pass)
