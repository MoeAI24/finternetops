Track B • Consultant

Consultant Track

For advisory partners delivering workshops and executive guidance. Translate Finternet Ops into governance, risk framing, and rollout plans without over-promising or crossing legal lines.

Open Onboarding Compliance Explainer Dashboard

Requirements

  • Runs discovery workshops and produces a structured problem statement.
  • Maps stakeholders, controls, and governance (auditability, explainability, retention).
  • Builds 30/60/90 roadmap with KPIs and pilot success criteria.
  • Delivers accurate executive briefs and safe positioning language.

Deliverables you must be able to produce

  • Discovery notes + executive-ready problem statement
  • 30/60/90 rollout plan with KPIs
  • Governance guardrails: what we do / don’t claim
  • Executive brief template populated for a pilot

Recommended learning path

  1. Foundations → pass quiz
  2. Complete your track’s core modules
  3. Use Partner Toolkit templates to build a pilot package
  4. Deliver a mock executive readout (15 minutes)
  5. Take certification exam (80% pass)
