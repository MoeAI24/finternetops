The Framework

This is a ladder of awareness. You don’t climb it with effort — you climb it by dropping what’s fake.

What it is

A practical way to describe how reality “feels” at different depths: from the body, to the mind, to the shared world, to the symbolic, to the cosmic, to the origin.

  • Designed for video + web storytelling
  • Clean language (no tech system diagrams)
  • Built to spark comments and self-reflection

What it’s not

Not an excuse to escape life. Not a hierarchy for ego. Not “spiritual flexing.” The point is clarity and alignment.

  • No “I’m above you” energy
  • No fear-farming
  • No confusion-for-profit
Remember: The closer you get to Source, the less noise you need. You don’t “win” the truth — you recognize it.

The Six Layers

Tap any layer to expand. The site remembers your last open layer.

1 Human Embodied life: sensation, survival, love, pain, choices. ⌄

The “street level.” Bills, relationships, hunger, joy, grief — and the raw moment where you decide what kind of person you are going to be.

Presence Integrity Courage Discipline
2 Mind Perception: thoughts, identity, filters, beliefs. ⌄

Here’s where stories become “truth.” You can be free… or trapped inside a narrative. This layer is about noticing the lens.

Attention Self-talk Bias Reframe
3 Collective Culture: media, tribe, language, “normal,” pressure. ⌄

The shared dream. This layer can uplift you — or program you. The question: Who benefits from what you believe?

Culture Power Narratives Discernment
4 Archetypal Symbols: patterns, myths, psyche, shadow, light. ⌄

Patterns underneath the pattern. The hero, the trickster, the tyrant, the redeemer — these show up in nations and in you.

Shadow work Symbols Myth Integration
5 Cosmic Order: intelligence, harmony, law, rhythm, design. ⌄

The feeling that reality has rhythm — and that your life can align with it. Less superstition, more resonance.

Resonance Synchronicity Order Silence
6 Source Monad / Pleroma: origin, totality, pure being. ⌄

Beyond language. Not a “thing” — the ground of everything. The closer you get, the more it becomes knowing instead of believing.

Monad Pleroma All-That-Is Unity
